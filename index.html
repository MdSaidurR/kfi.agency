Who We Are

At KFI Inovasi Sdn Bhd, we are more than a manpower agency—we are your trusted workforce partner. We specialize in the recruitment and placement of foreign skilled and semi-skilled workers across key industries such as manufacturing, F&B, construction, and agriculture.

We are a proud affiliate of Agensi Pekerjaan Jupiter Maju Sdn Bhd, a licensed manpower agency established in 2012 and approved by the Ministry of Home Affairs and Ministry of Human Resources, Malaysia. This strong foundation allows us to bring decades of expertise,compliance excellence, and a global recruitment network to every client we serve.

Manpower Solutions

We offer flexible models to suit diverse employer needs

Full Outsourcing

End-to-end solutions covering recruitment, documentation, housing, transport, medical, insurance, and worker welfare.

Partial Outsourcing

Recruitment & documentation only. Employer manages housing, transport, and welfare.

Direct Sourcing

Recruitment under client-approved permits. Employer retains full management while we ensure compliance and reliability.
